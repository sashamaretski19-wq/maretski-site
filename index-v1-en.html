About

Marketing as a system, not a set of campaigns

Over more than twenty years I have moved from launching brands on the market to leading marketing in e-commerce. Different industries — pharma, FMCG, distribution, retail chains, online retail — taught me one thing: sustainable growth doesn't come from flashy activations, but from a well-built system where every part works for revenue and the customer.

I think in numbers and cause-and-effect: not “reach went up,” but what it did for revenue, orders and the active customer base. A baseline, segment-level breakdowns, economic impact — for me this isn't reporting for its own sake, it's how you make decisions you can defend in front of the business owner.

I like building from scratch and putting order into processes — more than once I've set up a marketing team and its methodology where none existed.
